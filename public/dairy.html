Can Scrubber

Unique Engineers offer supreme quality Can Scrubber that is used for removing unwanted contaminants from the milk. Our can scrubbers are made from genuine stainless steel metal and are free from corrosion and leakage. These scrubbers comprises of motor, brushes, gear box and drive system. This equipment is available in standard and customized capacity as per the suitability of our clients. Our can scrubber is energy efficient and is stringently tested by our experienced engineers.

Unique Engineers offer supreme quality Dump Tanks that is used for food and dairy industries, we offer dump tanks that have a hygienic and sturdy structure. Unique Engineers manufacture hemispherical jacketed tanks used as Ghee Boilers. In the first jacket hot thermic fluid flows and in the outer jacket insulation is added to prevent heat loss.
